Kerala

Kerala, located in southern India, is known for its lush landscapes, backwaters, beautiful beaches, rich cultural heritage, and vibrant traditions like Kathakali and Onam, making it a popular tourist destination.

Pondicherry

Pondicherry, a coastal town in southern India, blends French colonial architecture with Indian culture. Known for its serene beaches, vibrant markets, Auroville, and rich history, it offers a unique travel experience.

Chennai

The Sri Ranganatha Swamy Temple in Srirangam, Tamil Nadu, is a significant Vaishnavite shrine dedicated to Lord Ranganatha. Known for its grand Dravidian architecture, it’s a major pilgrimage site and a Divya Desam.

Festivals & Food

Mysore Dasara

Mysore Dasara is an annual festival in Mysuru, Karnataka, celebrating the victory of Goddess Chamundeshwari over demons. Known for its grand procession, cultural events, and vibrant celebrations, it’s a major cultural event.

Explore
Chithirai Thiruvizha, Madurai

Chithirai Thiruvizha in Madurai celebrates the divine wedding of Goddess Meenakshi and Lord Sundareswarar. It features grand processions, cultural events, and attracts millions of devotees annually.

Explore
Dosa

Dosa is a popular South Indian dish made from fermented rice and urad dal batter. This thin, crispy pancake is typically served with chutneys and sambar. It’s a common breakfast or snack, enjoyed across India and globally for its delicious taste.

Explore
Idli

Idli is a traditional South Indian dish made from a fermented batter of rice and urad dal. These soft, fluffy steamed cakes are typically served with chutneys and sambar, making them a nutritious, light, and widely enjoyed breakfast or snack.
